🔬 Two-Group T-Test Comparison Explorer

Understand how t-tests compare the means of two independent groups

📊 Hypotheses

H₀ (Null): μ₁ = μ₂ (No difference between group means)

H₁ (Alternative): μ₁ ≠ μ₂ (There is a difference between group means)

Group Distributions Comparison
T-Distribution with Test Statistic
Welch's t-test formula:
t = (x̄₁ - x̄₂) / √[(s₁²/n₁) + (s₂²/n₂)]
where x̄ = sample mean, s² = sample variance, n = sample size

🔵 Group 1 Parameters

Mean (μ₁) 50
Standard Deviation (σ₁) 10
Sample Size (n₁) 30

🔴 Group 2 Parameters

Mean (μ₂) 55
Standard Deviation (σ₂) 12
Sample Size (n₂) 35

📈 Test Results

t-statistic: -1.23
Degrees of freedom: 63
p-value: 0.223
Effect size (Cohen's d): 0.31
